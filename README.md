# JustCal-Legal

JustCal AI Privacy Policy
Overview: JustCal AI is committed to your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data on our own servers.

Data Storage: All meal logs, nutritional data, and photos are stored locally on your device.

HealthKit: JustCal AI integrates with Apple Health (HealthKit) to write calorie data to your health dashboard. This data is never sent to our servers or shared with third parties.

Camera: The camera is used solely for analyzing food. Photos are processed and stored only on your device.

Subscriptions: Payments are handled securely by Apple and RevenueCat.

Contact: For support, contact us via this GitHub repository.
